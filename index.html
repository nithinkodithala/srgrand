Our Story

A Passion for Food,
A Commitment to Quality

Nestled in the heart of the city at RTC Bus Stand, SR Grand Family Restaurant has been serving authentic, heartfelt food for our beloved community. We believe every meal should be a celebration.

From traditional Telangana curries to Mughlai biryanis, from sizzling tandoor platters to crispy Indo-Chinese starters — our kitchen brings the richness of India's diverse culinary heritage to your plate.
